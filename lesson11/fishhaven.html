Autumn Market Day!

by Bruce Wayne

The semi-annual market day is scheduled for November 28th. If you have goods you would like to sell, make sure you submit an application at the courthouse before Wednesday. All buyers welcome! Bring your children to participate in the petting zoo. This year's zoo will feature ducks, cows, goats, horses, and Gilly the Tiger! After the zoo, head over to the Fry-shack for some sugary-sweet fry bread! Cotton candy will also be served after the firework show. Bring blankets and a heavy winter jacket as all activities will take place outside!
