Winter Preparedness

by: Bruce Wayne

Winters are the harshest time of the year. It is essential for you and your families safety to be prepared. Winter storms can bring cold temperatures, power failures, loss of communication services, and icy roads. To do list:

  • Verify operation of furnace.
  • Prepare a safe secondary method for heat.
  • Replace batteries on smoke and carbon monoxide detectors.
  • Inspect your chimney or flue.
  • Create an emergency car kit.
  • Check Casting-Fore forecast often to be up-to-date!
